Recognizing the Signs

Common symptoms of PCOD & PCOS

🔄 Irregular Menstrual Cycles

Infrequent, irregular, or prolonged menstrual periods are the most common sign of PCOS.

⚖️ Weight Management Issues

Difficulty losing weight or unexplained weight gain, particularly around the abdomen.

💇‍♀️ Excessive Hair Growth

Hirsutism - unwanted hair growth on face, chest, back, or other areas.

🌟 Skin Problems

Acne, oily skin, dark patches (acanthosis nigricans), or skin tags.

💆‍♀️ Hair Loss

Thinning hair or male-pattern baldness on the scalp.

😓 Chronic Fatigue

Persistent tiredness, low energy levels, and difficulty concentrating.

😔 Mood Changes

Anxiety, depression, mood swings, and emotional difficulties.

🤰 Fertility Challenges

Difficulty getting pregnant due to irregular ovulation.
